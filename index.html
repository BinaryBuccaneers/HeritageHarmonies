About Us

The original problem that led to the idea of preserving cultural music around the world is the gradual loss of traditional music and cultural heritage due to globalisation, modernisation, and other factors. As the world becomes more connected and cultures blend together, there is a risk of losing unique musical traditions that have been passed down for generations. This can result in a loss of cultural identity and diversity, as well as a lack of appreciation and understanding of the importance of cultural music. The project ’Heritage Harmonies’ aims to address this problem by documenting and sharing traditional music from around the world to ensure that it is not lost to future generations.
